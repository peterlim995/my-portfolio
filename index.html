ABOUT ME

Do you want to know about me?

My name is Peter Lim. I have BS in Computer Science and Information. After graduation, I taught the computer subject in the middle school. I also served in the Korean army for 3 years as a signal officer. I came to America in 2008 as a student. I got married in 2010. Since then, I have 5 daughters. I was main care giver of the children. But at the same time, I have been working and studying in many areas in this time, such technical support, Korean school teacher, daycare manager, etc. I also almost finished the master’s course in Biblical counseling.

Once I wanted to be a programmer and finished java program course in the Middlesex County College. But I couldn’t continue to build my career at the time because of family situation. Now I decided to challenge the new chapter of my life: becoming a full-stack web developer through Rutgers coding bootcamp. I am very excited to learn new skills and technics. I am familiar with coding and have many experiences. I have solved many problems in life through the research and challenge. I fix my car and most of home appliances by myself. I am a problem solver. I certainly believe that I can be a great web developer.
